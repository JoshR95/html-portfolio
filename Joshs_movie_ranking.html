My Top 3 movies of all time

Lord of the rings

Amazing movie about adventure, fantasy and action. a MUST watch.

Troy

One of my favourite films set in ancient Greece that is based around the seige of Troy. A fantastic film staring Orlando Bloom and Brad Pitt, with very good actions scense throughout.

Hercules

For me this arguably the best disney film ever produced. I am quite biased towards ancient Greece which makes this more enjoyable for me. However this film is sprinkled with great action, humour and superb stroy telling
